I love tatoos!

Hello! My name is Jessy Zhou and I'm currently a 22 year old tattoo artist. I'm currently working on small tattoos until I have more experience to work on bigger projects.

Jessy Zhou


Welcome to my portfolio where I display the work that I've done for my clients and work that is still up for grabs! I have many tattoos on my body and which goes to show how much love I have for tattoos. Currently, I just do small tattoo's that I draw up myself, which we refer to as "flash tattoos".
